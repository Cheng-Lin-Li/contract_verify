MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

1. Payment. Customer shall pay each invoice on net-45 day terms from the
invoice date. Late amounts accrue interest at 1% per month.

2. Limitation of Liability. Each party's aggregate liability under this
agreement is capped at the total fees paid in the twelve months preceding the
claim. Neither party caps liability for its own willful misconduct.

3. Confidentiality. The parties agree to mutual confidentiality obligations
that survive termination of this agreement for a period of three years.

4. Governing Law. This agreement is governed by the governing law of the State
of California, without regard to conflict of laws principles.

5. Term and Termination. Either party may terminate for material breach on
thirty days written notice.

6. Entire Agreement. This document constitutes the entire agreement between
the parties and supersedes all prior discussions.
